Welcome to the world of Tulip!
Tulips are erect flowers with long, broad, parallel-veined leaves and a cup-shaped, single or double flower at the tip of the stem.

Why?
Colors of the flowers can range anywhere from red to yellow to white.Some tulips are varicolored as a result of a viral disease carried and transferred to the plants by aphids.

Tulips promote regeneration and can be used for the repair and growth of tissues throughout the body and are required for the production of collagen. image and video
